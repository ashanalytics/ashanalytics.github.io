Innovative Technology for Actuaries

Using machine learning to transform financial modeling.

Made by Actuaries

Insurance companies are some of the world's largest producers and consumers of data. Better technology helps them use it.

Actuaries are responsible for parsing troves of data to model financial risk, design new products, and keep their companies in business.

However, technology is often a limiting factor. Actuaries rely on overburdened Excel models and proprietary, closed-source software with limited flexibility.

Meanwhile, the machine learning and artificial intelligence revolution continues to drive new technology forward.

Ash Analytics deploys the latest innovations from Silicon Valley so actuaries can make the most of their data.

Pricing Exotic Options in PyTorch

Automatic differentiation can be used to calculate the sensitivities, or "greeks", of a stock option, even if we use monte carlo techniques to calculate option price. Many exotic options can only be priced using monte carlo techniques, so automatic differentiation may be able to provide more accurate sensitivities in less time than traditional methods.
